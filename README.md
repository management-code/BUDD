## Our Products

BUDD offers a wide range of cannabis products designed to meet the needs of New York City residents. Our flower selection includes classic strains like Sour Diesel and OG Kush as well as unique cultivars developed by our in house breeding team. These strains are grown in controlled environments to ensure purity and potency. Each batch is tested by third party laboratories to confirm cannabinoid content and to verify the absence of contaminants. We also offer pre rolls that provide a convenient way to enjoy our flower. These pre rolls are crafted with premium ground flower and designed to burn evenly. Our edibles range from artisanal chocolates inspired by Fifth Avenue chocolatiers to gummies that evoke the playful spirit of Coney Island. Our gummies are made with natural fruit flavors and are available in low and moderate doses to accommodate different tolerance levels. BUDD tinctures offer a discreet option for those who prefer oral consumption. These tinctures come with droppers that allow precise dosing and can be added to beverages or food. We also produce topicals such as balms and lotions that can be applied to the skin to relieve soreness.

In addition to traditional cannabis products BUDD explores innovative formats. We offer cannabis infused teas and coffees inspired by local cafés in neighborhoods like Bed Stuy and Bushwick. Our beverage line includes sparkling water infused with terpenes that are reminiscent of walks through the High Line or afternoons in Riverside Park. We are exploring collaborations with breweries in Queensbridge to develop cannabis beverages that highlight the craft beer culture of New York City. Our product development team is constantly researching new extraction techniques such as rosin pressing and solventless extraction to create concentrates that appeal to experienced consumers. We also provide customizable subscription boxes that allow customers to receive a selection of products tailored to their preferences and delivered directly to their homes. These boxes may include items inspired by the neighborhoods where the subscribers live making each box feel personal and connected to the city.
